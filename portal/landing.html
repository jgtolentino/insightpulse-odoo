Frequently asked questions

Quick answers to common questions about InsightPulseAI. For anything more specific, we're happy to talk.

What is a delegated AI executor? ❯

It's an isolated environment that performs actions on behalf of an agent under strict policies. Instead of exposing your real infrastructure directly to models, executors mediate access and log every step.

How is InsightPulseAI different from a typical dashboard? ❯

Traditional dashboards show metrics; InsightPulseAI combines live topology, execution traces, and security policies into one interactive map. You don't just observe the system—you steer it.

Can we run InsightPulseAI in our own cloud? ❯

Yes. Enterprise Mesh deployments support private VPC or on-prem, with your own logging, monitoring, and key management systems.

Which model and tool providers are supported? ❯

InsightPulseAI is provider-agnostic. Connect OpenAI, DeepSeek, self-hosted models, HTTP APIs, databases, and internal tools through the same routing and policy engine.
